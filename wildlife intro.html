Wildlife in Sri Lanka


Explore Wildlife Reserves

Discover some of the most renowned wildlife reserves and national parks in Sri Lanka, offering unparalleled opportunities to observe the island's diverse wildlife amidst stunning natural landscapes.

Location Animals Found Interesting Facts/Tips
Yala National Park Leopards, Elephants, Sloth Bears - Renowned for high leopard density
- Best for morning safaris
Wilpattu National Park Leopards, Elephants, Sloth Bears - Largest national park in Sri Lanka
- Offers camping options
Minneriya National Park Elephants, Deer, Birds - Famous for the "Elephant Gathering"
- Visit during dry season for best sightings
Udawalawe National Park Elephants, Water Buffalo, Birds - Known for large herds of elephants
- Elephant transit home nearby
Horton Plains National Park Leopards, Sambar Deer, Birds - Offers panoramic views at World's End
- Leopards are elusive, best chance during early morning
